# Home_Assignments
Home_Based_Individual_Projects
## Project1: Smart Room Climate Monitor
 A live temperature & humidity display that warns you when your room gets too hot or humid.
## Project2: Gas & Fire Safety Alert
 A dual-sensor safety device that detects gas leaks and fire simultaneously — practical for any home.
## Project3: Ultrasonic Parking Assistant
 A real car parking sensor — distance-based LED + buzzer alert with OLED readout.
## Project4: Bluetooth Home Light Controller
 Control your room lights from your phone via Bluetooth — no Wi-Fi needed.
## Project5: Pressure & Altitude Logger
 Predict local weather using the BMP280 sensor and track atmospheric pressure changes over time. 
## Project6: Stepper Motor Precision Positioner
 A stepper motor with exact step precision — foundation of CNC machines, 3D printers, and robotic arms.
## Project7: Full IoT Home Automation Hub
 Combine everything you've learned — sensors, display, relay, Wi-Fi, and MQTT — into one complete smart home system.
